I ran into issues when putting links on each page. All the links worked on the home page, but when I went to put them on the other pages if I clicked on the link it would give me an error. I figured out that it was due to having a "." in front of the url. I also ran into an issue when trying to adjust the spacing between the links. I tried putting them in a div with a clss name, but it still wasn't working. I finally tried to style the a tag itself and it worked right away. I plan to look into this more to see why this is.

CSS Transition

In order to create my CSS transition I knew I wanted to change the title on my homepage's color and size. I chose this because that page has the least going on and thought it would add a little suprise without distracting from the content on the page. I had already styled the title a little before adding the transistion, so I knew that I just needed to had the transistion-property, transition-duration, and transition-delay. I first put in a bright color, made the delay short, and had the duration longer so that I could see if it was working correctly. Once I saw that it worked I adjusted this to match the aesthetic I wanted. I also decided that I wanted to change the font size, and for that I just added another transistion-property set to font size. The other key piece to this was having another h1{} in the stylesheet, but now I had h1:hover{} as I wanted the transistion to happen when someone hovers on the title.
